ABOUT

What is Lisbon Chillout Tour?

Hi and welcome to the official Lisbon Free Walking Tour website!

We are a team of creative and free-minded tour guides who work as a part of United Europe's independent network of free walking tours throughout the continental Europe!

If you support sustainable tourism or you're just looking for a tour free of formalities, free of commissions, free of pressure and full of authentic experience - just show up to our meeting point or book your group for a private tour around Lisbon!

How does it work?

Just show up to the meeting point at Praca Luis de Camoes (Luis de Camoes Square) and look for our guides - they are using a Recycled Silver bag with a tram on it.

The tour will be held even if just one person shows up.

For groups of people larger than 4 we ask you to send us a notice with the date of arriving so that we can make your experience more enjoyable and avoid too many groups of people coming at one time.

Send us an email in case you are brave enough to show up on a stormy day.

This is a
name-your-own-price walking tour so don't forget to tip your guide before leaving!

You are free to leave the tour at any point, however you should notice the guide before leaving out of courtesy.

No upfront cost.
You can join the tour no matter what your budget is. We do not want to exclude anyone.

Bring some snacks, water and a jacket for the afternoon.

Enjoy the tour and feel free to ask any questions!
