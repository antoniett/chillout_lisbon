THE ORIGINAL LISBON CHILL-OUT TOUR

Duration: 3h
Meeting point: Praca Luis de Camoes

This is a Lisbon Chill-out tour as it was when it all started in 2007. A backpackers' hit ever since, it is a tour without a fixed itinerary, where the walkers and the guide work out a route right before the start.

We design this tour by including a more realistic side of Lisbon, let us choose for you and show you why we love our city. Lets step off Rua Augusta, walk through quiet side lanes, peek into small streets smelling like codfish, sample local cafes and shops which we visit and enjoy the city through our eyes of a Lisbon resident.

THE LISBON LITERATURE FREE WALK

The tour happens on Wednesdays, Saturdays and Sundays and requires booking at least 72h before.

This tour shows a personal and local perspective of Literature through an alternative insight of the city. The Literature Tour is a journey across the works of writers inspired by Lisboa. This includes Portuguese icons like Fernando Pessoa, Eca de Queiroz and Jose Saramago, as well as foreign authors such as Erich Maria Remarque and Antonio Tabucchi.

It's not necessary to read any books in order to participate in the tour. You only need a passion for Literature and Art in general.

Meet our Guides

Ines

The passion for architecture, art and history drove her to seek some new ways of sharing this feeling of wonderful Lisbon and its unique light and colour that not everyone notices. With a masters degree in architecture and a curious mind for other cultures she likes to explore the city from a different perspective every day!

Luis

Committed in making them happy, he found a way to fulfill his dream as a tour guide. He considers himself a lifetime student who loves history, tales, culture, nature, backpacking, wine... Pedro

Born in Lisbon (well, at least that is what his mother told him..) this "alfacinha" (or “little lettuce” as people from Lisbon know to call each other) throws himself into many activities, with music being the biggest love of his life. Just don’t say we didn’t warn you if he starts singing or playing one of his many instruments on the tour!

Nuno
